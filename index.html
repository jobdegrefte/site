Home

I am a career track assistant professor at the University of Groningen. I work on business ethics, sustainability, and finance, with a research profile grounded in analytic normative philosophy. My current work focuses on responsibility, public accountability, and the ethical and epistemic norms governing financial institutions and markets.


I host an internal seminar series in Business Ethics at the Faculty of Economics and Business. I am a member of the Rudolph Agricola School Business and Human Rights group, the FEB Sustainable Finance research cluster, and the University of Groningen Centre for PPE.
